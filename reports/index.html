Reports

Original, sourced analysis on town budgets, regional school operations, state legislation, and other matters that come up in public deliberation.

Each report below is a longer, footnoted piece of work intended to inform a specific public conversation. Methodology and known data gaps are described inside each report; download links to underlying data accompany most of them.

About these reports. These pages are part of andoverct.info, a personally owned domain that is not an official site of the Town of Andover. Each report is independent research compiled by a private resident; sources are cited inline. Nothing here is official town communication, and nothing here is legal advice.
